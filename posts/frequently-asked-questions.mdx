## What is Musik?

[Musik](https://Musik.xyz/invite) is a Discord bot that revolutionizes the way you experience music on Discord, offering the cleanest user interface, high-quality audio, and a wide range of music-related features.

### How can I add Musik to my Discord Server?

Please [click here](https://Musik.xyz/invite) to check out our guide on adding Musik.

Yes, all Musik's settings will be saved.

## Limitations/Restrictions

### Can I change Musik's Profile Picture/Avatar in my server?

No, Discord does not allow bots to change their profile picture/avatar per server.

### Can I save or create playlists with Musik?

Yes! Musik supports the creation of custom playlists. Find out more about it [here](https://Musik.xyz/commands).

### What music sites does Musik currently support?

### Can I make custom commands for Musik's?

No, we do not allow custom commands or aliases.

### Can Musik stream the video of the song that is being played?

No, Discord does not allow bots to use the `Go Live` feature.

### Can Musik play music 24/7?

Yes! And it's **100% free** to do so. Just use the `me 247 <enable/disable>` command.

### Can Musik execute commands given by other bots?

No, Musik doesn't respond to commands issued by other bots as it can be easily abused. This restriction will not be removed, nor will any exceptions be made.

### Can Musik play music in Stage Channels?

Yes, just make Musik a Stage Moderator. All commands work exactly the same when you use Musik in a normal voice channel.
